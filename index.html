01 / ABOUT ZENOFAB

Engineering Precision.
Building Partnerships.

Founded in 2025 and headquartered in Mumbai, Zenofab Private Limited stands as a leading manufacturer and dedicated supplier of both standard and highly customized fasteners for critical industrial applications.

Our mission extends beyond simply supplying parts — we are a comprehensive Manufacturing & Design services provider, specializing in connecting complex engineering requirements from global clients with India's best production capabilities.

Our approach is built on speed, precision, and reliable supply chain management, making us the preferred "Build to Print" partner for clients in demanding regions, particularly Europe and the USA.

Vision

To become a trusted global partner for precision-engineered components — recognized for reliability, innovation, and quality.

Mission

To deliver end-to-end manufacturing and supply chain solutions that enhance our customers' competitiveness through precision and responsiveness.
